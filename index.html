Meet Joe

Photo of Joe

I'm Joe. I've been obsessed with clean cars since I got my first bucket and sponge at age 12. What started as me detailing every car in my family's driveway turned into a full-blown business.

I'm a one-man operation — which means when you book, you get me. Not some random guy. I show up with all my gear, you point me at your car, and I make it look like it just rolled off the showroom floor.

I serve all of Rhode Island. Just provide water and electricity, and I'll handle the rest.
